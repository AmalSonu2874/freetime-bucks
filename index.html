BASICS OF ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE



Intelligence is just problem solving and decision making .


GOALS OF AI :


  • Replicate human Intelligence.
  • Solve Knowledge intense task.
  • An Intelligent connection between perception and action.
  • Learn things by itself through learning.
  • Building a machine which can perform tasks that require human intelligence.

    • CORE CONCEPTS OF AI


  • MACHINE LEARNING :- Algorithms learn from data without being explicitly programmed .
  • NEURAL NETWORK :- These are networks of algorithms that mimics the way neurons interact , allowing computers to recognize patterns and sole common problems in AI .
  • DEEP LEARNING :- A subset of ML . It uses complex neural networks with many layers to analyse various factors of data .
  • NATURAL LANGUAGE PROCESSING : It involves programming computers to process and analyse large amount of natural language data , enabling interactions between computers and humans using natural language
  • ROBOTICS : While often associated with AI robotics , merges , AI Concepts with physical components to create machines capable of performing a variety of tasks , from assembly lines to complex surgeries .
  • COGNITIVE COMPUTING : This AI approach mimics human brain processes to solve complex problems , Often using pattern recognition , NSP and data mining .
  • EXPERT SYSTEM : These are AI Systems that emulate the decision making ability of a human expert applying reasoning capabilities to reach conclusion .

    • TYPES OF AI SYSTEM


  • NARROW AI : It is also known as ANI (Artificial Narrow AI) , refers to AI systems designed to handle a specific task or a limited range of task
  • GENERAL AI : Refers to AI system that posses the ability to understand , learn and apply intelligence across a broad range of tasks . memoring human cognitive abilities
  • SUPER INTELLIGENT AI : Represent an AI that not only mimics but significiantly surpasses human untelligence across all fielfds of science , general wisdom , social skills and more ....
